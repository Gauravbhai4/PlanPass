# BBMP/GBA FAR (Floor Area Ratio) Reference

## What FAR Means

FAR (Floor Area Ratio) = Total Built-up Area / Plot Area

If FAR = 1.75 on a 1,200 sq ft plot, you can build up to 2,100 sq ft of total floor area across all floors.

## Base FAR Limits by Zone (Residential)

These are general numbers under the Revised Master Plan 2015 with 2026 amendments. Exact values depend on plot size and road width.

| Zone | Use | Base FAR | Notes |
|------|-----|----------|-------|
| R (Single-family residential) | House | 1.75 | For most plots up to 4,000 sq ft |
| RM-1 (Mixed residential, low density) | Multi-family | 2.25 | Higher floors permitted |
| RM-2 (Mixed residential, high density) | Multi-family | 2.75-3.25 | Apartment-grade |
| C (Commercial) | Shop/office | 3.25-4.0 | Higher with premium FSI |
| Mixed-use | Shop + residential | 2.75 | Ground floor commercial allowed |

## Premium FSI

For an additional fee (premium charge), builders can purchase EXTRA FSI beyond the base limit:

- Premium FSI is sold in slabs (e.g., +0.5, +1.0, +1.5)
- Rates vary by zone and road width
- Common for commercial and high-density residential

## Common FAR Violation Patterns (Avoid These)

1. **Forgetting to include staircase cores and lift wells** in the FAR calculation
2. **Counting parking incorrectly** — covered parking is exempt up to a limit; uncovered is fully exempt; check the specific exemption clause
3. **Cantilevered projections** — balconies up to 1m projecting outside the setback line may be exempt, but anything more counts toward FAR
4. **Mezzanine floors** — partial mezzanines count toward FAR proportionally
5. **Terrace structures** — closed terrace rooms (penthouse) count; open utility rooms may be partly exempt

## Calculating Allowed Built-up Area

```
Plot Area (sq ft) × FAR = Maximum Built-up Area (sq ft)

Example:
1,200 sq ft plot × 1.75 FAR = 2,100 sq ft maximum built-up area
```

Then divide across floors based on the footprint (plot area minus setbacks).

## Quick Examples

### 30x40 Plot (1,200 sq ft), Residential R Zone
- Plot area: 1,200 sq ft
- FAR: 1.75
- Max built-up: 2,100 sq ft
- Setback-reduced footprint: ~720 sq ft per floor
- → Roughly G+2 fully built, or G+3 with smaller upper floors

### 30x50 Plot (1,500 sq ft), RM-1 Zone
- Plot area: 1,500 sq ft
- FAR: 2.25
- Max built-up: 3,375 sq ft
- → G+3 typical
