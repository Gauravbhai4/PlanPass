# BBMP/GBA Setback Quick-Reference Table (2026)

This is a fast lookup table for residential setbacks under the January 2026 amended Zonal Regulations.

## Small Plots (Up to 600 sq ft)
| Setback | Minimum |
|---------|---------|
| Front | 0.75 m (2.5 ft) |
| Side (either left OR right) | 0.6 m (2 ft) |
| Rear | NOT REQUIRED |

## Plots Up to 150 sq.m (~1,615 sq ft)
| Setback | Range |
|---------|-------|
| Front | 0.9 m - 1.5 m |
| Side (each) | 0.6 m - 0.9 m |
| Rear | 0.9 m - 1.5 m |

Actual setback values within ranges depend on building height (G+1, G+2, G+3, G+4).

## Plots > 150 sq.m
Setbacks are calculated as a percentage of plot dimensions and increase with building height.
- General rule: ~10-15% of plot width for each side
- Front setback typically 3.0-4.5 m
- Rear setback typically 3.0 m+

## Critical Compliance Rule

**Reducing a setback by even 0.6 m can trigger:**
- Plan rejection at first review
- BBMP penalties (₹50K - ₹2L+)
- Forced reconstruction costing ₹12 lakh or more

This is the #1 cause of rejection. Always verify setbacks against the actual plot survey (not just the architect's drawing).
