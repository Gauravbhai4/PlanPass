# Karnataka Zonal Regulations 2026 (Amended)

**Effective Date:** January 5-6, 2026
**Source:** Government of Karnataka notification, November 2025
**Legal basis:** Karnataka Town and Country Planning Act, 1961; Revised Master Plan (RMP) 2015 amendments
**Authority:** Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA), which replaced BBMP under the Greater Bengaluru Governance Act, 2024 for many approval functions

## Key Changes from Earlier Rules

### Setbacks (residential)
- Minimum **front setback: 0.75 m (2.5 ft)** for sites up to 600 sq ft
- Minimum **side setback: 0.6 m (2 ft)** — required on EITHER left or right (not both for small plots)
- **No rear setback** required for sites up to 600 sq ft
- For plots **up to 150 sq.m**: setbacks are fixed (0.6m-0.9m)
- For larger plots (>150 sq.m): setbacks are calculated as percentages of plot dimensions
- Setbacks increase with building height — refer to setback-table skill

### Building Heights
- **Stilt + 4 floors** now permitted on more plot sizes (previously G+3 for most small plots)
- Heights also depend on road width — see decision-tree-router skill

### Road Width Relaxation
- Construction allowed on roads as narrow as **12-15 feet** (previously 30-foot minimum)
- This significantly opens up small-lane residential construction

### Parking (NEW 2026)
- **EV parking** now MANDATORY for new commercial and residential buildings
- Visitor parking provisions added
- Mechanized parking exempts the conventional ramp requirement
- Per-unit parking ratios apply by zone — see parking-norms skill

### FAR (Floor Area Ratio)
- FAR limits revised upward in commercial and mixed-use zones to promote vertical growth
- **Premium FSI** available on payment of premium charges (rates vary by zone)
- For RM-1, RM-2 residential zones: base FAR depends on plot size and road width

### Occupancy Certificate (OC) Exemption
- Houses up to **1,200 sq ft** are EXEMPT from the OC requirement
- This is significant: OC was previously required before water (BWSSB) and power (BESCOM) connections
- Small-house owners can now get utility connections without OC

## Zones

BBMP/GBA divides the city into 8 administrative zones, each with subtle practice differences:
- Yelahanka, Mahadevapura, Bommanahalli, Rajarajeshwari Nagar, Dasarahalli, East, South, West

Land-use categories include:
- **RM-1, RM-2**: Residential — multi-family/mixed
- **R**: Residential — single-family
- **C**: Commercial
- **I**: Industrial
- **PSP**: Public/Semi-Public
- **TR**: Transport

## Compliance Notes

- For B-Khata properties: NO building plan can be approved. Must convert to A-Khata first.
- E-Khata / ePID has been MANDATORY since July 1, 2025 for all plan approvals.
- Under Sakala Mission, if BBMP/GBA does not respond within 30 working days, the plan is DEEMED APPROVED.
- New single-window timeline: 30-60 days for standard approvals.
